Wordle Vs.

How it Works

So, each turn you make a guess of a 5-letter word. Each turn you make you will find out what letters are in the word (Yellow) and which letters are in the word and the same location (Green). In this version of the game you have competition.

Each turn you make will be followed by the competitor, and it's your job to get the word first.

Good luck! But also enjoy.
